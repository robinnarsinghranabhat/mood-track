# Vibes AI — MANTRA 0.8

**MANTRA** = Multilingual Assessment of Neurocognitive Trajectories and Risk Analysis

## What It Does

Analyzes **how** someone speaks (not content) to detect cognitive/emotional state. Examines vocal patterns — pauses, rhythm, pitch variation, volume shifts, word selection, sentence complexity — to identify neurological signals.

## Key Specs

- Analyzes **500+ acoustic features**
- **80% detection accuracy** claimed
- Supports **15+ languages**
- Input: ~3 min natural speech sample
- Output: "Brain Readiness Score" + personalized recommendations + trend tracking
- Claims detection of brain changes up to **5 years** before clinical assessment

## Processing Pipeline

1. Natural Speech Capture (unstructured voice recording)
2. Digital Biomarker Extraction (500+ acoustic features)
3. Pattern Recognition (ML compares against personal baselines over time)
4. Output — Brain Readiness Score, trends, early warnings

## Scientific Basis

- Framingham Heart Study: 14 acoustic features significantly associated with dementia risk
- r=0.81 correlation between vocal features and cognitive assessment scores
- 77% ability to detect cognitive changes pre-symptom

Voice biomarkers can serve as a passive, low-friction data source during any voice conversation. Could extract emotional/cognitive state metrics from natural speech without requiring the user to self-report.
